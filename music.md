It's not all just data and public health! During the Covid-19 pandemic, my passion for piano transformed into a newfound love to produce Lo-fi music – a calm music genre mostly used by people to study and enhance productivity. My music has since then garnered almost a million listens on [Youtube](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCvVdaBSwXJXxhVhFfZa0Jrw) and [Soundcloud](https://soundcloud.com/loose_leaf_lofi).
