    <h2 style="margin-top: 0; font-size: 2rem; font-weight: 800;">La Carpio: Community Support Program</h2>
    <hr style="width: 60px; margin-left: 0; border: 1.5px solid #000; margin-bottom: 20px;">
    <p style="font-style: italic; font-size: 1.2rem; color: #444;">San Jose, Costa Rica</p>
    
    <p>During my time at United World College Costa Rica, I volunteered in La Carpio, the country’s most marginalized settlement. This experience reshaped my perception of Costa Rica, revealing the stark inequalities that exist even in the "happiest country on earth."</p>
    
    <p>To bridge cultural gaps, I fast-tracked my Spanish language acquisition, moving beyond simple tutoring to provide holistic support, including:</p>
    
    <ul style="padding-left: 20px; line-height: 1.6;">
      <li><strong>Healthcare Navigation:</strong> Assisting residents in accessing and navigating the national healthcare system.</li>
      <li><strong>Career & Growth:</strong> Leading career counseling and personal growth workshops.</li>
      <li><strong>Advocacy:</strong> Implementing anti-discrimination initiatives to support the local immigrant and refugee population.</li>
    </ul>
    
    <p>This two-way exchange of learning challenged my preconceptions and solidified my commitment to acquiring the skills necessary to build more equitable societies.</p>
